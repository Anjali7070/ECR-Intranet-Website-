DHANBAD DIVISION

Dhanbad division was initially one of the five divisions of the Eastern Railway but after the formation of East Central Railways on 1st October,2002, Dhanbad division became part of East Central Railway along with four other divisions.

PT. DEEN DYAL UPADHYAYA DIVISION

Pt. Deen Dayal Upadhyaya is a transit Division on New Delhi-Howrah route. This Division came into existence on 1st Jan,1978 and was known as Mughalsarai Division. It was renamed as Pt. Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Division on 15.01.2020.

SAMASTIPUR DIVISION

Samastipur Division came into existence on 1969. It serves 14 densely populated districts. Samastipur town is also a district head quarter, comprising of many sections of old Oudh Tirhut Railway apart from being a centre of rich cultural heritage of Mithila region.

SONPUR DIVISION

Sonpur division was established on 21 Oct. 1978 in North Eastern Railway zone. It follows the north side of the Ganges and the west side of the Gandak River. The division routes over 495.361 km.

DANAPUR DIVISION

Danapur railway division is one of the five divisions of East Central Railway (ECR), a zone of the Indian Railways located at Danapur in Patna district of Bihar state in eastern India. Danapur Division is an important passenger oriented division of East Central Railway. Its jurisdiction extends over a length of 752 route kms.
